# InOffice Help Guide

## Getting Started

Open the app and start tracking your office attendance. All data is stored locally on your device — no account or internet needed.

## Tabs

### Dashboard
- See today's date and whether you're marked for the office
- Tap **Mark Today as In Office** to log your day
- View your monthly progress ring and stats
- Toggle **Incl./Excl.** to see percentages with or without holidays/leaves
- Quick-nav cards to jump to Calendar, Insights, or Settings

### Calendar
- Navigate months with ← and → arrows
- **Tap** any day to open the status picker modal
- **Long-press** a day to quickly toggle between in-office / absent
- Status colors:
  - **White + green tile** = In office
  - **White (no tile)** = Absent
  - **Pink** = Public holiday
  - **Blue** = Personal leave
  - **Red** = Sick leave
- Tap **Jump to Today** to scroll back to the current month

### Insights
- View your current month's attendance percentage
- **Year to Date** card shows cumulative stats
- Month-by-month breakdown below
- Toggle **Incl. holidays / Excl. holidays** to switch views
- Use ← and → to change the year

### Settings
- **Target %**: Choose your monthly attendance goal (50%–100%)
- **Color Legend**: See what each cell color means
- **Import / Export**:
  - **Export to Excel** — saves your data as .xlsx to share
  - **Import from Excel** — load data from a previously exported file
  - **Download Sample** — get a template to fill in your data
  - **Format Help** — see the correct column layout for import files
- **Delete All Data** — wipes all attendance records and settings

## Excel Format

When importing or editing a spreadsheet, use this column layout:

| Date       | Status          |
|------------|-----------------|
| 2026-06-01 | in-office       |
| 2026-06-02 | absent          |
| 2026-06-03 | public-holiday  |
| 2026-06-04 | personal-leave  |
| 2026-06-05 | sick-leave      |

Valid statuses: `in-office`, `absent`, `public-holiday`, `personal-leave`, `sick-leave`

## Data & Privacy

- All data stays on your device
- No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking
- Delete all data anytime from Settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all data
